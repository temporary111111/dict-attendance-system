# Part 2: Speaking Script

## Slide 1 Script - Presenter 1

Good day po. We are student interns assigned to the Management Information Systems Service or MISS of DICT. For our major accomplishment, we planned and developed a Program and Event Attendance Monitoring and Reporting System. This presentation will briefly show the process we followed, the completed system, and the lessons we gained from the project.

---

## Slide 2 Script - Presenter 1

The project was based on DICT's existing paper-based attendance format, which collects attendee information, consent, and signatures during events. We identified an opportunity to make this process more convenient and organized through digital attendance collection, event monitoring, and automated report generation. Based on the requirements provided by our supervisor, we developed a system that supports this complete workflow.

---

## Slide 3 Script - Presenter 1

Before starting the development, we first analyzed the requirements and prepared the necessary system diagrams. The use case diagram helped us identify the users and their responsibilities. The data flow diagrams showed how information moves through the system, while the entity relationship diagram guided the organization of the database. These diagrams served as our foundation and guide throughout the development process.

### Handoff to Presenter 2

For the development and completed output, I will now turn the presentation over to my fellow intern.

---

## Slide 4 Script - Presenter 2

After completing the planning stage, we developed the system in several phases. We first finalized the requirements, implemented the main functions, and tested the complete workflow. Some requirements changed during development, so we also adjusted our design and implementation based on feedback. Through continuous testing and refinement, we were able to complete and polish the system.

---

## Slide 5 Script - Presenter 2

The completed system covers the full attendance process. Administrators can create and manage events, then share an automatically generated QR code or attendance link. Attendees can open the public page and submit their information, consent, address, and digital signature. Administrators can then monitor the submitted records, manage their attendance status, and generate an official attendance sheet after the event.

The system also provides separate access and responsibilities for the Super Admin and Program Admin, helping keep event and attendance management organized.

---

## Slide 6 Script - Presenter 2

Through this project, we successfully completed a functional web-based attendance monitoring and reporting system for DICT. More importantly, we experienced the complete software engineering process, including requirements analysis, system planning, database and interface design, development, testing, and refinement.

We also improved our teamwork, problem-solving, communication, and ability to adapt to changing requirements. This experience allowed us to apply our academic knowledge to an actual organizational need and contribute to DICT's continuing digitalization efforts.

That concludes our presentation. Thank you po.
